Overview

At Epicodus, you have a few options to choose from when it comes to what language you want to learn. Each language has their own unique syntax as well as different career opportunities once you graduate the program.

Directly below are the 3 different tracks we offer and some descriptions on each.

Once you've gotten yourself aquainted with the different languages, and their frameworks, go ahead and fill out the questionnaire to see what the best fit is for you!

Ruby/Rails
  • Builds interactive web applications.
  • Used for accounts, entering information, and interacting with dynamic content.
  • Most popular with startups and smaller companies who are looking to build their product quickly.
JavaScript/React
  • Coined as "The Language of the Web."
  • Used to make HTML/CSS websites more dynamic.
  • Web Developers, Frontend Engineers, and folks making web applications use this language .
C#/.NET
  • Ss an object-oriented programming language backed by Microsoft.
  • The language behind many desktop applications.
  • Typically, used for building internal software for big companies.
